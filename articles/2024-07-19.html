2nd Anniversary Resolution

Fluxus Cantonia has been operating as an ''art collective'' in the last two years; this has turned out to be not ideal for future plans. Thus, the following decisions have been made:

  • A new political party would be formed:
    • The name of this political party shall be Action Party Cantonia (APC for short).
    • This political party shall be standalone, i.e. the party would not be a part of Fluxus Cantonia and any operation of the party from this point forwards will have no predetermined relationship to Fluxus Cantonia.
    • The main purpose of this party is to fully inherit the de facto political party function Fluxus Cantonia has been holding during the last two years of its operation.
    • Starting from the 2nd Anniversary of Fluxus Cantonia, its current only member, Setsuna K, shall become its provisional representative; the process of actual establishment of the party shall officially begin on August 21st August, 2024.
    • The full formal name of reference, when referring to both Fluxus Cantonia and the new party, shall be Fluxus Cantonia / Action Party Cantonia (FC/APC for short).
